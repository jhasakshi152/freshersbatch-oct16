LOVELY PROFESSIONAL UNIVERSITY

WELCOME VERTO

Lovely Professional University is a private university situated in Jalandhar, Punjab, India.

The university was established in 2005 by Lovely International Trust, under The Lovely Professional University Act, 2005 and started operation in 2006.

The Lovely Institute of Law was ranked 18th among the 18 by India Today's "India's Best Colleges 2017: Law".
